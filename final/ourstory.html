Our Story

At the heart of Pacific Trails Resort is a love story.

Hi there, we're John and Amy! Years ago, we met while staying at a peaceful coastal retreat. We were both experiencing stressful events in our lives and were seeking a pause from the fast pace of everyday life. In that moment we not only found each other, but ourselves. The retreat was just what we needed. Inspired by that experience, we created Pacific Trails Resort as a tribute to what brought us together.

Whether you are here to celebrate and connect or heal and simply rest, we hope Pacific Trails Resort becomes part of your story.

Welcome to our little slice of paradise. We're so glad you are here!

Our Values

We strive to provide an exceptional resort experience that blends comfort and luxury - ensuring every guest leaves with meaningful experiences and a renewed sense of well-being.

  • Delivering warm and personalized service that makes guests feel cared for.
  • Upholding the highest standard in comfort, cuisine, and guest experience.
  • Promoting balance and rejuvenation through nature, relaxation, and mindful living.
